Legend of Zelda Tower Defense

This was a massive project split with 3 people between 1 computer programmer, 1 artist, and 1 3D modeler. This project was a 4-player online multiplayer game that utilized a VPS for hosting game servers and a database to contain server information for clients to retrieve to connect to the game server. Additionally, PHP and shell scripting was utilized to make life easier in the VPS side of things. Unfortunately, this project was canceled due to poor programming strategies and the scope of the limitations of the project. Here are some pictures and a link to our youtube down below!

Project Aetheris

Project Aetheris is a singleplayer 3D open-world fantasy-rpg game that has been on and off in production over the past 3 years. It is a 4 person project split between 1 computer programmer, 1 artist, 1 3D modeler, and 1 music producer. Development is not expected to be picked back up at a constant pace until Q2 2025.
